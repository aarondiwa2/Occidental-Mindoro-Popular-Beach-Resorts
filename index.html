Mamburao

Vista Del Mare Beach Resort

Vista Dal Mare Beach Resort is one of the most exquisite and popular resorts in the town of Mamburao. With beautiful sceneries, Four pools varying in height and access to the seaside shore. Want to spend your afternoon here? Come rent one of the cottages fit for your liking! Want to spend the night? Why don't you come rent a room to continue the fun in the morning! Have fun swimming in the pools and at the beach! Take a walk, play volleyball at the seashore, or watch the sunset! Come have a good time at Vista Dal Mare!

Paluan

La Virginia Beach Resort

La Virginia Beach Resort Paluan is located at Barangay Tubili, Sitio Absukot, Paluan. And They invite you to spend time with them in their humble resort!

Rent our rooms to your liking at the seaside. Eat meals with your family in their outside dining areas. Let the fresh air help you and your group relax! Take a swim at their pool and give yourself a great time!

San Jose

Inasakan Beach Resort

Inasakan Beach Resort is one of the most idealic place to spend your vacation when you're in San Jose! Located in San Jose, Occidental Mindoro, this beach is the right place to take your family and friends on a fun beach in the summer!

Take a walk on their white-sand beach while watching the clear waves! Want to take your family on a boat ride out into the sea? Go on a day tour and breath in the fresh air of the ocean! Interested in taking a hike and sight seeing? Come along for an adventure and take walk to see different landscapes in the tour!
